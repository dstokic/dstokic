Esther's Success in Regards to Her Children

It is no suprise that someone who is as high-achieving as Esther has children who are the same, but it is still astounding just how successful they all are.

Susan Wojcicki

Susan attended Harvard University for her undergraduate degree, was involved in the founding of Google, and currently is the CEO of 23andme

Janet Wojcicki

Janet attended Stanford University for her undergraduate degree and is currently an anthropologist and epidemologist who is currently an associate professor at UCSF

Anne Wojcicki

Anne attended Yale university for her undergraduate degree, became a health care analyst for a few companies before eventually co-founding the successful business 23andme
